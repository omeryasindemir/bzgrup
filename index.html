About Us

We act as a bridge between our production partners and our business partners abroad.

We coordinate and execute export consultancy, foreign trade intelligence, competitor analysis, transportation, customs clearance processes of our production partners in the furniture industry.

We choose to refer our customers as our business partners.

What we do for our business partners?

First of all; We are negotiating and working with our production partners on issues such as quality, pricing, production plan and delivery time about product requests.

After that; We follow up on quality control, on-time production, loading and shipment during the production phase.

And, we continue to support after sale (SAS).

Our aim is to establish trust-based and long-term relationships.

Dear business partners, we are here for you…
